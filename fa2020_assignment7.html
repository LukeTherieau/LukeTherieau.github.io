#This bar chart compares the number of games published by the top two video game publishers (by number of games published) and by the top two genres .

  1. Make a plot use geom_col.
#This column chart shows the top 5 genres (by total games published) and compares their average global sales revenue per game.
df %>% group_by(Genre) %>% filter(Genre == "Action"|Genre == "Sports"|Genre == "Misc"|Genre == "Role-Playing"|Genre == "Shooter") %>% summarise(mean=mean(Global_Sales)) %>%

labs(
